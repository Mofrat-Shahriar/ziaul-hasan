Biography

Ziaul Hasan is a distinguished retired civil servant of the Government of Bangladesh, belonging to the 8th Batch of the prestigious BCS (Administration) Cadre. With a rare academic foundation spanning both engineering and governance, he served the nation for over 34 years in diverse and consequential roles — from grassroots Upazila administration to the pinnacle of national policy-making as Senior Secretary.

Born on 15 September 1964, he earned his B.Sc. in Civil Engineering from Bangladesh University of Engineering & Technology (BUET) — laying a strong analytical foundation — before later completing an M.A. in Governance and Development from BRAC University with a CGPA of 3.68, reflecting his lifelong commitment to intellectual growth alongside public service.

Leadership Philosophy

Throughout his career, Ziaul Hasan embodied a philosophy of servant leadership: placing the welfare of ordinary citizens — particularly the poor and underprivileged — at the centre of every policy decision and administrative action. He firmly believed that governance reform must be driven not by top-down mandates alone, but by genuine empathy and structured stakeholder engagement.
